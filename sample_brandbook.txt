# TechFlow Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview
TechFlow is a modern SaaS platform for workflow automation. We help teams work smarter, not harder.

## Brand Personality
- Innovative
- Trustworthy
- Approachable
- Efficient
- Human-centered

## Brand Colors

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Blue**: #4F46E5 (Indigo)
- **Secondary Purple**: #7C3AED (Violet)
- **Accent Orange**: #F59E0B (Amber)

### Neutral Colors
- **Text**: #111111 (Almost Black)
- **Background**: #FFFFFF (White)
- **Gray**: #6B7280 (Cool Gray)

Minimum contrast ratio: 4.5:1 for all text

## Typography

### Heading Font
- **Family**: Inter
- **Weights**: 700 (Bold)
- **Sizes**: 48px, 36px, 28px

### Body Font
- **Family**: Inter
- **Weights**: 400 (Regular), 600 (Semi-bold)
- **Size**: 16px

## Logo Guidelines

### Logo Usage
- Minimum size: 128px width
- Clear space: 1x logo height on all sides
- Allowed positions: Top-left, Top-right, Bottom-right
- Never distort, rotate, or outline the logo

### Logo Variants
- **Full color**: Use on light backgrounds
- **White**: Use on dark backgrounds
- **Monochrome**: Use when color is not available

## Layout System

### Grid
- 12-column grid system
- Gutter: 8px
- Outer padding: 40px
- Border radius: 16px for buttons and cards

## Voice & Tone

### Voice Characteristics
- Professional but friendly
- Clear and concise
- Action-oriented
- Empowering, not demanding

### Forbidden Terms
Never use these words:
- "cheap"
- "discount"
- "revolutionary" (overused)
- "limited time only"
- "act now"
- "don't miss out"

### Approved Call-to-Actions
Only use these CTAs:
- "Get Started"
- "Try Free"
- "Learn More"
- "Join Now"
- "Sign Up"
- "Start Now"
- "Discover"

## Content Guidelines

### Headlines
- Maximum 7 words
- Lead with benefit, not feature
- Use active voice
- Be specific

### Subheads
- Maximum 16 words
- Expand on headline
- Include social proof when possible
- End with a question to engage

### Button Copy
- 2-3 words maximum
- Action-oriented verbs
- Use approved CTAs only

## Social Media

### Instagram
- Square (1:1): 1080x1080px
- Portrait (4:5): 1080x1350px
- Story (9:16): 1080x1920px

### LinkedIn
- Single image: 1200x627px
- Carousel: 1080x1080px

### Facebook
- Feed image: 1200x630px
- Story: 1080x1920px

## Visual Style

### Photography
- Natural, authentic imagery
- Diverse representation
- Avoid stock photo clichés
- Minimal staging

### Illustrations
- Clean, modern line art
- Use brand colors
- Consistent style across assets

### Icons
- Outlined style (not filled)
- 2px stroke weight
- Rounded corners

## Brand in Action

### Example Good Headlines
- "Work Flows Better"
- "Automate Your Workflow"
- "Save 10 Hours Weekly"
- "Built for Modern Teams"

### Example Bad Headlines
- "The Revolutionary New Platform" (forbidden term)
- "Don't Miss This Limited Offer!" (forbidden terms)
- "Cheap Automation Tools" (forbidden term)
